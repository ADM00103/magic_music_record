About Us

Magic Music Record

A Russian music label as well as a community dedicated to the best of the best electronic tracks. Magic Music Record releases are free to listen to, and some to download. On the market for over 3 years. Over 1000 tracks released. We have been working since 2018.
Why Us? An excellent team of professionals, publishing tracks, creating covers, creating Merch and much more. Fast royalties payouts! (Listening, downloading illegal use, for commercial purposes.) And all this in a short time, and most importantly, completely free. Waiting for you in our team, professional and novice musicians!


Who is the founder? Oskin Daniil (born March 24, 2000), better known as Magic Music Record, Russian aspiring musician and label founder.
