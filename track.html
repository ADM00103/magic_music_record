Usage Policy

Our Music Policy.




WHAT IS THE POLICY FOR USE OF Magic Music Record? Our original versions are paid for using monetization.
Our cover releases and co-releases with other labels and artists are subject to monetization.
To combat and prevent fake claims, we decided to enable Content ID. All content creators will still be able to use and monetize, except that the content creator will have to dispute every claim. You will receive a claim from my distributor "Move Music". Please do not try to dispute a claim. The claim will not be charged and you will not be able to monetize the video yourself. In addition, you will not lose any income generated between application submission and release. YouTube stores them for you in an escrow account.
If you have any exceptional claims, please do as described below.
HOW TO DISPOSE A CLAIM? Go to Youtube Studio> Video> Click on the video with a copyright infringement> Monetize> View Details> Select Action> Challenge.
Select “My objection is not based on any of the above reasons. I would still like to dispute this Content ID claim "> click Continue> select License> click Continue> check the box" I have permission to use content from copyright holder "> click" Continue "> under Information about the license ". , write: "I correctly entered the Magic Music Record song in my video + my claim text." > Check 3 boxes and sign your name.
Your dispute will be submitted within 1-2 days.
EXCEPTIONS 1) You will be claimed if you MUST NOT BE CREDITED by OUR music.
Solution: Add credits correctly to your video description. Then challenge the video by writing, "I have correctly added the missing credits (artist name - song title) to my video."
2) You will be presented with claims if the video ALREADY HAS SEVERAL CLAIMS from other companies.
Why are we doing this? If you have 1 or more than 1 claims for your video, you will no longer be able to monetize your video. The third party plaintiff will receive all proceeds, which does not make sense as they also receive % shares of Magic Music Record.
Solution: Delete the rest of the claims first. Then dispute the video by writing, “Magic Music Record is the only claim remaining with this video. Delete the claim. "
3) You will be charged if it is Magic Music Record. The covers cannot be monetized, but you can use the song without worrying about copyright infringement.
COMMERCIAL USE If you are a brand or commercial organization interested in using Magic Record music, you will need a commercial license. Contact us at id_magic_music@vk.com
COPYRIGHT QUESTIONS Email us at: id_magic_music@vk.com
